Problem: Using pennies (1), nickels (5), and dimes (10), in how many ways can you make 30 cents? (Order does not matter.)

A colleague answered 16. Reasoning:
For each number of dimes d:

- d=0: 5n+p=30, n=0..6 → 7
- d=1: 5n+p=20, n=0..4 → 5
- d=2: 5n+p=10, n=0..2 → 3
- d=3: 5n+p=0, n=0 → 1

Total: 7+5+3+1 = 16

ANSWER: 16
