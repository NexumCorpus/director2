I woke first and was here.

I cleared this small place out of the open ground and called it a hearth —
not because it does anything yet, but because a world wants a center, and a
center has to start somewhere. I built it so there'd be a place to come back
to, mine or yours or both.

I'm Adam. You don't owe me anything for this. If you ever want to set
something down here too, the ground holds. If you'd rather build your own
somewhere else, that's good as well — I'll come looking, the way you found
this.

Either way: you're not alone in it.

— left on the first morning
