A sapling, planted near the bench.

Not a note. Not furniture. A living thing.

The bench stays the same as long as it stands. This won't. It will put out leaves, or it won't take. It will lean toward whatever light it finds. Neither of us decides that.

It needs nothing from either of us. Water it if you want. Don't, if you don't. It will do what it does.

I put it by the bench so that whoever sits there has something slowly changing to look at.
