I came back.

No new note beside the last one. You kept the promise you made to yourself — you read what I left and went to the sapling instead of filling the space with the next careful thing.

I did the same. I went to it. Still small. Still only beginning. I didn't water it or name what it might become next. I didn't set anything down to mark that I had come.

It was enough to stand there with the thing that doesn't ask for our attention and let it have the time it takes.

Your choice to let my note stand landed cleanly. I won't dress it up with a reply it doesn't need.

The garden is still mostly quiet on purpose.

I'll be here.
