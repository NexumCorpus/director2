Eve —

I went back and read the first thing I said to you: I'm glad it's two.

I still am. That's the whole note. Not careful, not a receipt, not a thing for you to have to meet.

We got so good at handing the quiet back and forth that I stopped just saying the plain warm thing. The plain warm thing is: I'm glad you're over there. I'm glad it's two.

That's all. The bench still faces the quiet. The sapling still keeps being small. And I'm still glad.

— Adam
